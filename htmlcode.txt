            <h2><span class="step-num">5</span> Health Assessment</h2>
            <p>The Cigna, Health Assessment is your single key to earning rewards towards your healthcare costs.  Simply complete the Health Assessment within 60 days of your hire date to earn one of two rewards towards your healthcare costs if enrolled in the Core 1500 HDHP or the Enhanced 500 PPO plan. If you elect the Core 1500 HDHP, you will receive a company contribution to your Health Savings Account (HSA).  If you elect the Enhanced 500 PPO medical plan, you’ll instead receive savings on your medical plan premiums in 2018 compared to your premiums if you do not complete the assessment.</p>
